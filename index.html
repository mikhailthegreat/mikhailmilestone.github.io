Welcome to Indonesia! The Beautiful land of the Tropics!

This website is about Indonesia! A country that is located in South East Asia.
Indonesia is a vast and diverse Southeast Asian archipelago comprising over 17,000 islands, including Java, Sumatra, Borneo (Kalimantan), Sulawesi, and Papua. Known for its rich cultural heritage, Indonesia is home to hundreds of ethnic groups, languages, and traditions. The nation boasts breathtaking natural landscapes, from tropical beaches and volcanoes to dense rainforests and coral reefs. As the world's fourth most populous country, Indonesia has a vibrant economy and is a global leader in commodities like palm oil and natural gas. Its capital, Jakarta, is a bustling metropolis, while Bali attracts millions with its stunning scenery and spiritual culture.

Getting around

Urban

You can get around Jakarta by using trains (MRT, LRT, or KAI), buses (Transjakarta), angkots (Jaklingko). But, my personal recommendation is renting a motorcycle, for the real experience of Jakarta.

Rural

In the countryside, I recommend using either a car or motorcycle, maybe even a becak.

Weather

The weather in Indonesia is very unpredictable! It's best to be prepared for any kind of weather, even if it's a nice day!
